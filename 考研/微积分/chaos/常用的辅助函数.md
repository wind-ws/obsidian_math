

### 乘积求导

1. $[f(x)f(x)]'=[f^{2}(x)]'=2f(x)f'(x)$
    见$f(x)f'(x)$,
    作$F(x)=f^{2}(x)$
2. $[f(x)f'(x)]'=[f'(x)]^{2}+f(x)f''(x)$
    见$[f'(x)]^{2}+f(x)f''(x)$,
    作$F(x)=f(x)f'(x)$
3. $[f(x)e^{g(x)}]'=[f'(x)+f(x)g'(x)]e^{g(x)}$
    见$f'(x)+f(x)g'(x)$,
    作$F(x)=f(x)e^{g(x)}$
    见 $f'(x)+f(x)$,作$F(x)=f(x)e^{x}$
    见 $f'(x)-f(x)$,作$F(x)=f(x)e^{-x}$
    见 $f'(x)+kf(x)$,作$F(x)=f(x)e^{kx}$
