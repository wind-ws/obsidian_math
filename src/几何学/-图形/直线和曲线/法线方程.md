

曲线在点$(x_{0},y_{0})$的法线方程
$\displaystyle{y-y_{0}=- \frac{1}{f'(x_{0})}(x-x_{0})~,~f'(x_{0})\neq 0}$

若$\displaystyle{x-x_{0}=0}$,则$\displaystyle{f'(x_{0})=0}$
若$\displaystyle{y-y_{0}=0}$ ,则$\displaystyle{f'(x_{0})=\infty }$
