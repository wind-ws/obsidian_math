### 积分的线性性质
$k_{1}$,$k_{2}$为常数
$$
\int_{L}^{ } (k_{1}F_{1} \pm k_{2}F_{2})\cdot dr=k_{1}\int_{L}^{ } F_{1}\cdot dr \pm  k_{2}\int_{L}^{ } F_{2}\cdot dr
$$
